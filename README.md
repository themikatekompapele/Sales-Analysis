# RETAIL SALES ANALYSIS
Analysis of daily trading information for a large retail store, the primary objective is to  develop metrics and derive subsequent insight.

# Objective

- Daily sales price per unit
- Average unit sales price of this product
- Daily % gross profit
- Daily % gross profit per unit

# Key Insights and Deliverables

## Summary
 - R186.9 Million generated in total revenue
 - 5M Units Sold
 - Average Daily Unit Price : R37.07

## Sales Trend
- Total Sales and Cost of Sales increase and decrease together at the same time, in the last three years, a gap exists between total sales and cost of sales - an indication of decline in sales(low profitability).

## Sales Performance Over Time ( Total Units Sold vs. % Average Daily Gross Profit)
- Low % Average Daily Profit when total units sold are higher.

## Price Elasticity of Demand
- The year 2015 (Quarter 4)  has an outlier of -149.46 PED, due to seasonal demand, or product promotions.However, the product is price sensitive.
